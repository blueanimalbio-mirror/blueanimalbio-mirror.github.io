　　海雕亚科Haliaeetinae有2属10种。大型，常在海岸、湖泊、河流附近活动，捕食鱼和水鸟，也吃腐肉。包括白腹海雕Haliaeetus leucogaster、所罗门海雕Haliaeetus sanfordi、非洲海雕（吼海雕）Haliaeetus vocifer、马达加斯加海雕Haliaeetus vociferoides、玉带海雕Haliaeetus leucoryphus、白尾海雕Haliaeetus albicilla、白头海雕Haliaeetus leucocephalus、虎头海雕Haliaeetus pelagicus、渔雕Ichthyophaga humilis、灰头渔雕Ichthyophaga ichthyaetus。

Haliaeetinae

 

 

 

 

 
海雕属 Haliaeetus (fish eagles)
白尾海雕 Haliaeetus albicilla (white-tailed eagle)
玉带海雕 Haliaeetus leucoryphus (Pallas's fish eagle)
白腹海雕 Haliaeetus leucogaster (white-bellied sea eagle)
白头海雕 Haliaeetus leucocephalus (bald eagle)
虎头海雕 Haliaeetus pelagicus (Steller's sea eagle)
所罗门海雕 Haliaeetus sanfordi (Sanford's fish-eagle)
非洲海雕（吼海雕）Haliaeetus vocifer (African fish eagle)
马达加斯加海雕 Haliaeetus vociferoides (Madagascan fish )
鱼雕属 Ichthyophaga (lesser fish eagles)
渔雕 Ichthyophaga humilis (lesser fish eagle)
灰头渔雕 Ichthyophaga ichthyaetus (grey-headed fish )
